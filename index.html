Basic Self Defense Techniques

Learning basic self-defense can give you the confidence and skills to protect yourself in dangerous situations. Here are some fundamental techniques every woman should know:

Target Vulnerable Areas

Aim for eyes, nose, throat, knees, and groin. Even a small person can inflict serious pain on these areas.

Palm Strike

Use the heel of your palm to strike the attacker's nose or chin. This can create distance to escape.

Escape First

Your primary goal should always be to escape, not to fight. Use techniques to create an opportunity to run.

Make Noise

Yell "Fire!" instead of "Help!" as it draws more attention. Use a personal alarm if available.
